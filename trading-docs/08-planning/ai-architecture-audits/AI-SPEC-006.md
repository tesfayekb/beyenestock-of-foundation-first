## 2. Spec Intent Summary (verified by GPT)

**(First-pass draft for GPT-5.5 Pro to refine.)**

AI-SPEC-006's load-bearing architectural commitment is its **first-paragraph framing** at locked-spec line 14: the meta-labeler is a **`Bayesian-shrunk, sample-efficient, calibration-first filter`** that "rejects weak rules-proposed trades and reduces size on uncertain proposals" and explicitly does NOT "generate alpha, override the AI Risk Governor, increase risk beyond rules/Governor caps, or bypass constitutional risk gates." The architectural keystone (Spec §14 lines 772–776) is four-pronged: (1) AI controls admissibility and size ceilings, not trade execution; (2) rules engine is the safety floor; (3) constitutional gates are non-overridable; (4) caps compose by **minimum**, never by averaging. The technical core is a three-part hybrid probability `p_take = w_prior * p_prior + w_model * p_logistic + w_memory * p_memory` (Spec §2 line 161) with sample-size-dependent weights — at `n_train < 150`, prior dominates at 0.55; at `n_train >= 300`, logistic dominates at 0.50 (Spec §2 lines 165–181). The Bayesian prior is hierarchical with smooth pooling across 5 levels (`global` / `strategy` / `strategy×regime` / `strategy×regime×time_bucket` / `strategy×regime×time_bucket×event_bucket`) using formula `p_level = (wins_level + k * p_parent) / (n_level + k)` with `k=20` (Spec §2 lines 185–199). The training target is **strategy-specific utility** (Spec §1 line 32) — per-strategy quality scores `U_IC`, `U_IB`, `U_PCS`, `U_STRADDLE`, `U_DCS` enforce hurdle rates plus penalties for breach proximity, time decay, MAE, and event-day short-gamma exposure (Spec §1 lines 44–144). The feature vector reads from FIVE producer surfaces (Spec §3): Item 5 EV Table (18 fields), Rules Engine (5 fields), LightGBM Direction Model (5 fields), AI Risk Governor structured outputs (9 fields), Memory Retrieval Top-20 Analogs (10 fields) — totaling 47 features plus 4 derived features. Authority promotion (Spec §10 lines 626–673) is graduated: 50 candidates → advisory; 100 candidates + 20 actuals → reduced; 200+ candidates + 50 actuals + ≥20 per strategy → production skip/reduce; 300–500 candidates + 100+ actuals → full 1.0 sizing authority. Day-1 V0.2 authority is **`advisory or reduced authority only`** with hard constraints "cannot increase size", "cannot enable long-vol live", "cannot override Governor veto" (Spec §10 lines 666–673). Expected ROI contribution months 7–12 at maturity is **+3% to +7% annual full-account ROI** (Spec line 7). The architectural role per spec line 8: "the keystone — sits between Item 5's strategy_ev_table and the AI Risk Governor, deciding which proposed trades actually get taken." This makes Item 6 a **cross-spec consumer of Items 1, 2, 4, 5, 7 (Memory Retrieval — un-audited)** and a **cross-spec producer for AI-SPEC-002's `decision_outcome` enum** (per AI-SPEC-002 audit, Item 6 writes `blocked_meta_labeler` as a decision_outcome value — but the locked Item 6 spec is silent on this enum; see `B-AI-006-9` below).
